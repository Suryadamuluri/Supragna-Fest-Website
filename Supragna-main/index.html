About Supragna


SUPRAGNA, the prestigious national-level fest of Siddhartha Academy of Higher Education, Deemed to be University, celebrates the fusion of knowledge, creativity, and innovation. A vibrant platform for students to showcase their talent through diverse competitions, cultural events, and thought-provoking workshops, SUPRAGNA embodies the pursuit of wisdom and excellence.
